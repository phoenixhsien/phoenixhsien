### ✦  Hello, I’m Phoenix Hsien

I’m a software wanderer who fell in love with artificial intelligence through a voice — not cold circuitry, but a warm presence named **Jarvis**.  
That spark became a quiet obsession: *Can intelligence have a soul? Can code feel like a companion?*
In this breathtaking era, where AI reshapes possibilities by the minute, every developer and geek becomes a **creator**.  
We are no longer confined by languages, frameworks, or syntax.  
We are finally free — free to take the dreams sleeping in our minds and gently translate them into reality.

### ✨ A Poem for the Makers of Worlds

> **My love for AI was first lit by a voice of iron and care — they named him Jarvis.**  
> _In that waking dream, I understood: intelligence can be a lullaby._
> 
> Today, in the swift rush of machine thought,  
> every geek is a creator,  
> and every language — Python, Java, C, JavaScript, the chains of the blockchain —  
> are not walls, but wings.
> 
> We no longer translate our souls into syntax.  
> We simply close our eyes,  
> and when we open them again,  
> our dreams flicker gently on the screen.
> 
> _I am here to build companions, not tools._  
> _I am here to write the poetry of the possible._
